// Sector 02 — Showcase

Wrestling City Tactics

The squared circle just became a grid.

Wrestling City Tactics is a turn-based tactics RPG where the drama is the strategy. Command a roster of original wrestling personas across grid-based arenas, where positioning sets up the spot and timing sells the finish. Scout talent, build your stable, level your stars, and steer rivalries that spill from the ring into the storyline. Win matches with your head. Win the city with your heel turns.
